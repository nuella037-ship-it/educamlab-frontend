Your Privacy Matters

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Version: 2.0 | Effective Date: June 1, 2026

Quick Summary

EduCamLab collects minimal personal data (name, phone number, and learning progress) to provide educational services. We never sell your data to third parties. Your PIN and password are encrypted and stored securely. You can access, update, or delete your account at any time.

At EduCamLab, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. By using our platform, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

1 Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Full name, phone number, and password (hashed for security).
  • Usage Data: Course progress, quiz results, activity logs, and learning preferences.
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, device type, and operating system.
  • Payment Information: Transaction details (we do not store full payment credentials).
  • PIN Information: Your unique access PIN (encrypted and stored securely).
  • SMS Data: Phone number used for sending verification codes and notifications.
  • Communications: Messages, feedback, and support requests you send to us.

2 How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our educational services and course content.
  • To track your learning progress and offer personalized recommendations.
  • To send you important updates, verification codes, and notifications via SMS.
  • To process payments and manage your subscription plans.
  • To ensure platform security, prevent abuse, and enforce our Terms of Service.
  • To generate and manage your unique access PIN for secure login.
  • To analyze platform usage and improve user experience.

3 Data Protection & Security

  • All data is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols.
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text.
  • PINs are encrypted and stored securely in our database.
  • We use secure payment gateways (Orange Money, MTN Mobile Money) for all financial transactions.
  • Regular security audits are performed to ensure data integrity.
  • Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only.
  • We use two-factor authentication for administrative access.

4 SMS Communication

  • We use SMS to send you important account information and verification codes.
  • You will receive SMS notifications for PIN delivery and payment confirmations.
  • You may receive SMS updates related to your account activity and course progress.
  • Standard SMS rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier.
  • You can opt out of non-essential SMS communications at any time.
  • We use reputable SMS providers to ensure message delivery.

5 Your Rights

  • You can access, update, or delete your account information at any time.
  • You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
  • You can delete your account permanently by contacting support.
  • You can opt out of marketing and non-essential communications.
  • You can withdraw consent for data processing at any time.
  • You have the right to data portability (receive your data in a common format).

6 Data Retention

  • We retain your data for as long as your account is active.
  • After account deletion, we retain minimal data for legal and security purposes.
  • Payment records are retained for up to 5 years for financial compliance.
  • Usage logs are retained for 30 days for performance analysis.
  • Communication logs are retained for 1 year for customer support purposes.

7 Third-Party Services

  • We use Orange Money and MTN Mobile Money for payment processing.
  • We use SMS gateways for sending verification codes and notifications.
  • We use analytics tools to improve platform performance (anonymized data only).
  • We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • Third-party services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

8 Cookies & Tracking

  • We use essential cookies for authentication and session management.
  • We use analytics cookies to understand how users interact with our platform.
  • You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.
  • We do not use cookies for cross-site tracking or advertising purposes.

9 Children's Privacy

  • EduCamLab is intended for students aged 13 and above.
  • Users under 13 must have parental consent to use our platform.
  • We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 without consent.
  • If we discover data from a user under 13 without consent, we will delete it immediately.

10 Changes to This Policy

  • We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
  • Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
  • Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
  • For significant changes, we will notify you via SMS or email.
  • We will update the version number to track changes over time.

11 International Data Transfers

  • Your data is stored on servers located within Cameroon.
  • We do not transfer your personal data outside of Cameroon.
  • Third-party services may have servers in other countries. We ensure they comply with data protection standards.

12 Compliance with Cameroon Law

  • EduCamLab complies with the Cameroon Data Protection Law (Law No. 2010/012).
  • We follow guidelines from the National Commission for the Protection of Personal Data.
  • We are committed to protecting the privacy rights of all Cameroon citizens.

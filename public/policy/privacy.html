🔒 Your Privacy Matters

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

At EduCamLab, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, phone number, and password.
  • Usage Data: Course progress, quiz results, and activity logs.
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, and device type.
  • Payment Information: Transaction details (we do not store full payment credentials).
  • PIN Information: Your unique access PIN (encrypted and stored securely).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our educational services.
  • To track your learning progress and offer personalized recommendations.
  • To send you important updates and notifications via SMS.
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions.
  • To ensure platform security and prevent abuse.
  • To generate and manage your access PIN.

3. Data Protection

  • All data is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols.
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text.
  • PINs are encrypted and stored securely.
  • We use secure payment gateways for financial transactions.

4. SMS Communication

  • We use SMS to send you important information including PINs and payment confirmations.
  • You may receive SMS notifications related to your account activity.
  • Standard SMS rates may apply.

5. Your Rights

  • You can access, update, or delete your account at any time.
  • You can request a copy of your data.
  • You can opt out of marketing communications.

6. Contact Us

For privacy concerns or questions, please contact us.

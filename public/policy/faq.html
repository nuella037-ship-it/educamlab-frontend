❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions

What is EduCamLab? General

EduCamLab is Cameroon's leading exam preparation platform. We provide comprehensive resources for GCE (O Level and A Level), BACC, BEPC, and Technical GCE exams.

Who is EduCamLab for? General

EduCamLab is designed for students preparing for Cameroon national exams, including O Level, A Level, BACC, BEPC, and Technical GCE.

How do I create an account? Account

Click "Get Started" or "Create Account", fill in your details including name, phone number, and password. You'll receive a PIN from admin after payment confirmation.

What if I forgot my password? Account

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your phone number, and we'll send you a password reset link via SMS.

What is a PIN and how do I get it? PIN

A PIN is a 6-character code that allows you to access the platform. You receive your PIN from the admin after payment confirmation via SMS.

What courses are available? Courses

We offer resources for all Cameroon national exams including O Level, A Level, BACC, BEPC, and Technical GCE.

How much does EduCamLab cost? Payment

We offer flexible subscription plans: Daily (150 XAF), Weekly (800 XAF), Monthly (2,500 XAF), and Annual (15,000 XAF).

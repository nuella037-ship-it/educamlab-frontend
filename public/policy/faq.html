❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about EduCamLab

What is EduCamLab? General

EduCamLab is Cameroon's leading exam preparation platform. We provide comprehensive resources for GCE (O Level and A Level), BACC, BEPC, and Technical GCE exams. Our platform offers past questions, revision pamphlets, interactive quizzes, and progress tracking to help students excel in their national exams.

Who is EduCamLab for? General

EduCamLab is designed for students preparing for Cameroon national exams, including:

  • O Level (Form 4 & 5)
  • A Level (Lower & Upper Sixth)
  • BACC (Séries A, B, C, D)
  • BEPC
  • Technical GCE
Is EduCamLab free? General

EduCamLab offers both free and premium content. Registration and basic access are free. Premium features including full course access, past questions, and revision pamphlets require a subscription. We offer flexible plans starting from 150 XAF per day.

How do I create an account? Account

To create an account:

  1. Click "Get Started" or "Create Account" on the homepage
  2. Fill in your details: First name, Last name, Phone number, and Password
  3. Agree to the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy
  4. Click "Create Account"
  5. You'll receive a verification code via SMS
  6. Enter the code to verify your account
  7. After verification, contact admin via WhatsApp to get your PIN
What if I forgot my password? Account

If you forgot your password:

  1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
  2. Enter your 9-digit phone number
  3. You'll receive a password reset link via SMS
  4. Follow the link to create a new password
Can I delete my account? Account

Yes, you can delete your account at any time. Go to your Profile page and click "Delete Account" in the Danger Zone section. Please note that this action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed.

What is a PIN and how do I get it? PIN

A PIN is a 6-character alphanumeric code that allows you to access the platform. Here's how to get it:

  1. Create your account on EduCamLab
  2. Verify your phone number via SMS
  3. Contact admin via WhatsApp at +237 654 122 717
  4. Make your payment for the subscription plan
  5. Admin will send you your unique PIN via SMS
  6. Use this PIN along with your phone and password to login
What if I lost my PIN? PIN

If you lost your PIN, contact admin via WhatsApp at +237 654 122 717 or email support@educamlab.com. You will need to verify your identity to receive a new PIN. Please do not share your PIN with anyone.

How do I access my courses? Courses

Once you have an account and PIN:

  1. Log in with your phone number, password, and PIN

  3. Browse by exam level (O Level, A Level, BACC, BEPC, Technical GCE)

I'm having trouble logging in. What should I do? Technical

If you're having trouble logging in:

  1. Make sure you're using the correct phone number (9 digits)
  2. Check that your password is correct (case-sensitive)
  3. Verify that your PIN is exactly 6 characters (alphanumeric)
  4. Try resetting your password if needed
  5. Clear your browser cache and cookies
  6. Try a different browser or device
  7. If the issue persists, contact support at support@educamlab.com
I'm not receiving SMS verification codes. What should I do? Technical

If you're not receiving SMS verification codes:

  1. Make sure you entered the correct phone number (9 digits)
  2. Check that your phone has network coverage
  3. Try clicking "Resend Code" on the verification page
  4. Check if you have SMS blocking enabled on your phone
  5. Wait a few minutes and try again
  6. If the issue persists, contact support at support@educamlab.com
